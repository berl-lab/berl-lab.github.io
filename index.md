# BERL

## Bioenergy & Environmental Remediation Laboratory

Department of Resources and Environmental Engineering  
Hanyang University

BERL conducts interdisciplinary research to develop sustainable solutions for energy production, resource recovery, and environmental remediation.

Our research focuses on bioenergy, battery recycling, environmental remediation, and the recovery of valuable resources from waste materials.
