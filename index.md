# BERL

## Bioenergy & Environmental Remediation Laboratory

Department of Resources and Environmental Engineering  
Hanyang University

BERL (Bioenergy & Environmental Remediation Laboratory) conducts interdisciplinary research on sustainable energy production, environmental remediation, and critical resource recovery.

Our research integrates environmental engineering, bioenergy technologies, and battery recycling to contribute to a sustainable circular economy.
